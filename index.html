La Choza Mia

La Choza Mia is a catering service ready to bring a bit of crunch into your restaurants that will keep the people coming back for more! Their homemade delicacies leaving your customers happy and ready for more!

Empanadas are the current product being mass produced by this catering service. An empanada is a delicious, flaky pastry stuffed with different kinds of meats or sweet fillings. Made by a Dominican mom and daughter duo who started the company, these empanadas will take the joy used in the kitchen and spread it to peoples plates across the country. Some current options available include chicken, beef & cheese, fish, vegetarian, and a guava & cheese dessert empanada (which can be seen in the table below).

These ladies are teaming up with Bravo Foods in order to produce the quantity needed to put their delicious products in your restaurants. You don't want to miss out! For more details, contact us at lachozamia@gmail.com.

Type and Availability may vary depending on time of year. Options in red are our most popular.
